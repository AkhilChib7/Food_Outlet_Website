Our Menu

Gheur

Gheur is an authentic, traditional dogri recipe from Jammu and Kashmir. Dogri people usually prepare gheur when new bride enters the house for the very first time or someone who comes home after very long time. It is like a welcome dish.

Maani

Mani is a curry made with raw or half ripened mangoes, usually served with steamed boiled rice. Most of the regions have their own way of preparing Mani using different ingredients. Here is the recipe of Mahni from our place Jammu (Jammu & Kashmir) & is pronounced as Mani in Dogri accent.

Kimb

Kimb, called Citron or citrus medica, is a fruit cultivated in tropical regions.

Sund

‘Sund’, which is very similar to ‘Panjiri’, is very popular in the Jammu region because of its nutritional and medicinal properties.
